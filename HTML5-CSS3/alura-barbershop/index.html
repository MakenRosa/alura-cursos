About Alura Barbershop

Located in the heart of the city, Alura Barbershop brings the best for your hair and beard to the market. Founded in 2019, Alura Barbershop is already a standout in the city, attracting new clients every day.

Our mission is: "To provide self-esteem and quality of life to our clients."

We offer experienced professionals who stay up-to-date with changes in the world of fashion. Our service has a standard of excellence and efficiency, ensuring the quality and satisfaction of our clients.
